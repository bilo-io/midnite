# Office

## What it is

A **pixel-art floor** of your agents. Each agent has a desk; the floor is driven by
live presence, so you can see who's active and walk up to a desk to call or
message them. Office is **desktop-only** — it's gated behind the desktop breakpoint.

## Purpose

Office is the playful, spatial view of your fleet. Instead of a list, you get a
room you can move around; stepping up to a desk is a quick way to reach a specific
agent. It turns "which agents are running" into something you can see at a glance.

<Screenshot src={officeShot} alt="Office floor" caption="The Office — a pixel-art floor of your agents." />

## Using it

| Action | How | Behind the scenes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Walk the floor | Move around the room | presence-driven view |
| Switch views | View selector (`?view=`) | office views |
| Call / message an agent | Step up to its desk | presence + session |
| See who's active | Live presence on the floor | `getPresenceSummary` |

> Office requires a desktop-width viewport; on smaller screens it's hidden behind
> the desktop-only gate.
